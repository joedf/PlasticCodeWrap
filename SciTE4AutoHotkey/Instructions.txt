Copy "PlasticCodeWrap.style.properties" or "Monokai.style.properties" to
	"C:\Users\<username>\Documents\AutoHotkey\SciTE\Styles" or
	"SciTE\Styles"

Then, in SciTE4AutoHotkey, Select "Tools -> SciTE4AutoHotkey Setting...",
then set "Style" to "PlasticCodeWrap", click "Update" and restart
SciTE4AutoHotkey.
