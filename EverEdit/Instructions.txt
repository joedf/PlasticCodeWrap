Copy "PlasticCodeWrap.ini" to EverEdit\Theme Folder...
Then, Launch EverEdit and Navigate (in EverEdit) to "Tools -> Settings -> Theme"
and select "PlasticCodeWrap" and Click Apply/OK...

Optionally, You change the Font to:
Consolas, size 10   or    DejaVu Sans Mono, size 10
To change the Font, Navigate (in EverEdit) to "Tools -> Settings -> Font"
and Select the font, then click Apply/OK.
